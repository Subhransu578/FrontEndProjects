The echo of freedom, passions and experiences.

The music of the Land of Joy running 24/7. The Scrambler community taking the stage to share passions
and experiences. Radio Ducati Scrambler is where people meet, tell their stories and listen to great tunes!

World Radio Day 2019 – a symbol of dialogue, tolerance and sharing – is when the new Radio Ducati
Scrambler was launched: a brand new schedule of radio programmes featuring original contributions from
writers, travellers and musicians.

Listen to “From zero to hero”, the travel blog run by Riccardo Felici, to discover some of the world’s most
inspiring places. Hear the female point of view in Valentina RocketQueen and Federica Moschiano’s “Don’t
call me Cinderella”.

Tune in and meet famous sports champions and lesser-known heroes of the motorcycle world. And get in
touch with the wonderful Scrambler community: Radio Ducati Scrambler reserves plenty of space for
listeners to tell their own story and express their way of living.
